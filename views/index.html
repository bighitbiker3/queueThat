Directions

Here's how it works:

1. Put the end of any Soundcloud permalink you want in the search box above. For example, if the artist is http://soundcloud.com/duvetcover you'd put duvetcover in the search.

2. Pressing enter brings up that artists favorited tracks. If you want the tracks they've posted, click the Tracks :) button.

3. If you put in a valid search, the box will turn green and you won't see this anymore. Instead you'll see a list of tracks. It shows 200 results. If there are more, hit Next Page.

4. Click a track to add it to your queue, you can move it up and down or delete it from the queue. Just hover over it with your mouse. Enjoy :)))))
